What is the purpose of ATL lab in school About Atal Tinkering Labs

The objective of this scheme is to foster curiosity, creativity, and imagination in young minds; and inculcate skills such as design mindset, computational thinking, adaptive learning, physical computing etc.

What is the importance of ATL labs?

The chief objective of establishing ATLs is to promote curiosity, imagination, and creativity in the minds of school-going children. Other goals of the scheme include inculcating in children a design mindset, adaptive learning, computational thinking, physical computing, etc.
